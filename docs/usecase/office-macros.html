SignPath + Microsoft Office Macros

Office Macros - Magical Helper for Many, Immense Pain for Others

Magical Tool

Microsoft Office Macros are a very usefull tool to automate workflows in your documents and do boring repetetive tasks very quickly.

You also can interconnect the different Microsoft Office applications together and create unique user experiences for you and your collegues.

Immense Pain

Macros are programs within Office documents that execute as the user opening the document.

That makes Office macros a preferred way for hackers to get into organizations, with attacks ranging from ransomware to phishing.

Administrators find it almost impossible to provide policy settings that define which macros may be executed.

Use Macro Signing to Control which Macros are allowed to run!

Step 1

Use Group Policies to allow execution only for macros signed with trusted certificates

Step 2

Assign trusted certificates to users and groups

Step 3

Use SignPath to sign your reviewed Office Macros

Step 4

Only reviewed and signed macros will be executed in your whole organization
